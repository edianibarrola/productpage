Bonsai

$19.99

The purpose of bonsai plants are primarily for contemplation of the viewer, and the pleasant exercise of effort and ingenuity by the grower.

By contrast with other plant cultivation practices, Bonsai is not intended for food or medicine. Instead, Bonsai practice focuses on long-term cultivation and shaping of one or more small trees growing in a container.
